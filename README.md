## ğŸ“Š Key Findings

Summary from the EDA (see `notebooks/02_eda.ipynb` for full plots and insights):

| Finding | Summary |
|---------|---------|
| **Delay distribution** | Median arrival delay ~**13 min**; distribution roughly symmetric to mildly right-skewed, with most values 0â€“25 min and a long tail of severe delays. |
| **Temporal patterns** | Mean delay by hour and by day of week shows limited variation in this sample; peak vs off-peak and weekday vs weekend are similar (~13.3 min). |
| **Route/location** | **Route_12**, **Route_18**, and **Route_15** have the highest mean delays (~14 min); good candidates for operational focus. |
| **Correlations** | Delay correlates weakly with traffic congestion and precipitation; no single numeric feature is strongly linear with delay; categoricals (route, weather) may help modeling. |
| **On-time performance** | **~25%** of trips are on time (arrival delay â‰¤ 0); **~75%** are delayed. |
